# 📚 Example Use Cases & Prompts

## Sample Documents You Can Test With

### 1. Research Papers
**Best Prompts:**
- "What are the main findings of this study?"
- "Explain the methodology used"
- "What are the limitations mentioned?"
- "Summarize the conclusion"
- "What future research is suggested?"

### 2. Legal Documents
**Best Prompts:**
- "What are the key terms and conditions?"
- "Explain the liability clauses"
- "What are the termination conditions?"
- "Summarize the obligations of each party"
- "What are the payment terms?"

### 3. Technical Documentation

- "What are the system requirements?"

### 4. Business Reports
**Best Prompts:**
- "What were the quarterly results?"

### 5. Educational Materials
**Best Prompts:**
- "Explain this concept in simple terms"
- "What are the key takeaways?"
- "Create a summary for studying"
- "What are the main examples given?"
- "How does this relate to [topic]?"

## Tips for Best Results

### Do's ✅
- **Be specific**: "Explain the results of experiment 2" vs "Tell me about experiments"
- **Ask follow-ups**: Build on previous answers for deeper understanding
- **Request formatting**: "List the key points as bullet points"
- **Cite specifics**: "According to section 3, what does it say about..."
- **Ask for comparisons**: "How does X compare to Y in the document?"

### Don'ts ❌
- **Don't ask about information not in documents**: AI will say it doesn't know
- **Avoid overly broad questions**: "Tell me everything" - be more specific
- **Don't expect memorization**: AI uses citations, not perfect recall
- **Avoid questions requiring external knowledge**: Stick to document content

## Troubleshooting Your Queries

If you're not getting good answers:

1. **Make questions more specific**
   - Bad: "Tell me about this"
   - Good: "What are the three main recommendations in section 2?"

2. **Provide context**
   - Bad: "What does it say?"
   - Good: "According to the methodology section, what tools were used?"

3. **Ask one thing at a time**
   - Bad: "What are the results, limitations, and future work?"
   - Good: "What are the results?" (then ask about limitations next)

4. **Verify information is in document**
   - The AI only answers from uploaded documents
   - It will say "I don't have enough information" if data isn't present

5. **Use follow-up questions**
   - Build on previous answers for deeper analysis
   - Reference earlier responses: "You mentioned X, can you elaborate?"
